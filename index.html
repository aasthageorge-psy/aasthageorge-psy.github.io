Hi, I'm Aastha 👋

I'm a counselling psychologist who works with young adults navigating the messy, beautiful, sometimes-overwhelming chapters of life. Whether you're feeling stuck, anxious, or just need space to unpack your thoughts—I'm here to support you.

Areas of Support

  • Overthinking, Anxiety & Self-doubt
  • Burnout, Boundaries & Work-life stress
  • Relationships & Emotional patterns
  • Perfectionism & Self-criticism
  • Trauma-informed emotional care

Therapy Style

I combine evidence-based methods like CBT, Narrative Therapy, and DBT with a warm, collaborative approach. Think of it as a space where you're heard, not fixed. Your goals lead the way—we work together to get you there.
