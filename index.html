Stories We Can Rewrite Together

Every challenge you face has a story behind it. Here are some common narratives that we can work together to transform:

The "Not Good Enough" Story

When you're constantly telling yourself you're not smart enough, attractive enough, or successful enough. We'll explore where this story came from and rewrite it.

The "Always Messing Up" Story

When you believe you're destined to fail or make mistakes. We'll find the times you've succeeded and build a new narrative around your capabilities.

The "I Can't Change" Story

When you feel stuck in old patterns and believe change is impossible. We'll discover your agency and ability to create new possibilities.

The "I'm Too Much" Story

When you feel like you're too emotional, too needy, or too much for others. We'll explore what this story protects and find healthier ways to meet your needs.

The "I'm Not Worthy" Story

When you struggle to believe you deserve love, respect, or good things. We'll uncover your inherent worth and build a story of self-compassion.

The "I'm Alone" Story

When you feel isolated or believe no one understands you. We'll find the connections and support that already exist in your life.

How Narrative Therapy Works

Narrative therapy is a collaborative approach that helps you become the author of your own life story. Here's how we'll work together:

1

Exploring Your Story

We'll talk about the dominant stories in your life‚Äîthe ones that might be limiting you or causing distress. We'll identify where these stories came from.

2

Externalizing Problems

We'll separate you from your problems. Instead of "I am anxious," we'll talk about "the anxiety" as something separate from who you are.

3

Re-authoring Your Story

Together, we'll discover alternative stories‚Äîtimes when you've shown strength, resilience, or values that matter to you. We'll build a new, preferred narrative.

What Makes Narrative Therapy Different

Narrative therapy offers a unique approach to healing and growth:

You Are Not the Problem

The problem is the problem. We don't pathologize you or see you as broken. Instead, we work together to understand how problems have influenced your story.

Your Expertise Matters

You are the expert on your own life. I'm here to ask questions and collaborate, not to tell you what to do or how to fix yourself.

Stories Can Change

The stories we tell ourselves shape our reality. By rewriting these stories, we can create new possibilities and change how we experience our lives.
